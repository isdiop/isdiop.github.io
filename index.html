About Me

I am a Computer Science graduate from Université Laval with experience in both software development and functional analysis within complex systems. I have worked on designing, implementing, and validating software features in collaborative environments, including API-based and real-time systems.

On the development side, I work with Unreal Engine 5, Unity,Java,Pythpn C++, and C#, focusing on softwares, gameplay systems, architecture, and interactive prototypes.

On the analytical side, I have experience gathering requirements, documenting specifications, performing functional testing, and supporting users in production environments. I enjoy bridging the gap between technical implementation and functional needs by designing structured systems, solving complex problems, and iterating through testing and feedback to improve system quality and user experience.
