About Me

Hi! I'm a passionate game developer and a computer science student currently studying in Canada. Originally from Senegal, my love for technology and creativity drives me to explore various aspects of game development, from designing immersive gameplay experiences to crafting intricate game mechanics. I have experience working with Unreal Engine 5 and Unity, with a focus on creating enjoyable experiences and immersive 3D games. I’m particularly interested in developing unique game concepts, like turn-based strategies and interactive environments where physics play a key role. Beyond game development, I enjoy debugging and problem-solving, using tools like gdb to troubleshoot complex code. I'm also working on building my own portfolio, where I can showcase my evolving skills and projects. Feel free to explore my work and reach out—I'm always excited to connect with fellow developers and collaborators!
